Put your images and videos here.

Then point to them from ../js/data.js, for example:
  image:   "assets/img/cover.jpg"      (a home tile cover)
  poster:  "assets/img/summer.jpg"     (a still for a project)
  video:   "assets/img/summer.mp4"     (a project video — or paste a YouTube/Vimeo link)
  portrait:"assets/img/misha.jpg"      (your About photo)

Suggested sizes:
  - Vertical reels: 1080 x 1920 .mp4
  - Tile covers / posters: ~1200px on the long side, JPG or WebP
